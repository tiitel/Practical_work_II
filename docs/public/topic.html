TOP 3 2024 MEN COLOGNES

Nowadays the cologne industry is massive and entering in this "world" might be difficult , for this reason i leave you here my top 3 colognes to this day

  1. Y Eau de Parfum (Yves Saint Laurent)

    2. A classic, well known for it's versatility, making it possible to use it in any time of the ayer an for any occassion.
    It's fresh and clean, but it also includes a mature touch that suits it perfectly.
    It lasts about 6 hours depending on your skin and the quantity you use, but for a fresh fragance its all you could ask for

  2. Spicebomb extreme (Viktor & Rolf)

    3. This fragance, in my opinion, is the best winter-fall fragance that exist for a normal price and here is why.
    It's sweet since the main note is the vanilla one, you would think that this vanilla would smell like a woman,
    but spicebomb implement some tobacco and spicy notes that makes it perfect for a man
    This one lasts 7-8 hours so you can apply once a day you are going to smell for all the time you are so it's great

  3. The Omniscient Mr Thompson (Penhaligon's)

    4. This fragance its the definition of a winter fragance, it's practically perfect for this time of the year(if it wasn't for the price), while ,sadly,
    its impossible to wear in summer It has some vanilla too but this one, unlike spicebomb, has some indistinguishable iris and woody notes that makes it,
    for me, the best fragance of this year. It also last like 10 hours so you know you are paying for quality
